Smashed Burgers

Description

Smashed Burgers maximize the Maillard reaction to make for the best-tasting burger.

Ingredients

  • Martin's potato roll
  • American cheese
  • Ground beef 80/20

Steps:

  1. Form 2oz balls with ground beef.
  2. Place ball of meet onto griddle and smash down with spatula/smasher.
  3. Salt and pepper the patty and cook until browning starts to occur on the top side, approx 60-90 seconds.
  4. Finish the damn thing.
